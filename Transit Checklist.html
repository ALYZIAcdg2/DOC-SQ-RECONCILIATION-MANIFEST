Take over from Cabin Crew:
Met the aircraft and took over the responsibility for the UMNR from the cabin crew.
"Disembarkation - Cabin Crew Copy" of the UMNR Folder signed, extracted and retained by the Cabin Crew.
Took over UMNR Folder from cabin crew.
If UMNR travelling with parents/guardians in different cabin class and UM escort services has been declined:
Handed over the minor and UMNR Folder to parents/guardians.
Informed parents/guardians that the minor and UMNR Folder has to be handed over to ground staff handling connecting flight.
Transit formalities:
Assisted UMNR through disembarkation and re-embarkation formalities. (Security screening, etc)
UMNR was taken care of for the entire duration of the transit.
Escorted UMNR to gate early and introduce to Cabin Crew-in-charge (CIC) during boarding.
CIC acknowledged the handover of the UMNR by signing the "Embarkation-Ground Staff Copy" of the UMNR Folder.
"Embarkation-Ground Staff Copy" extracted and retained by the ground staff.
