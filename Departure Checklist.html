UMNR SELF-ASSESSMENT CHECKLIST (To be completed by handling agent)
Notes:
  • This checklist is intended for the sole purpose of ensuring Unaccompanied Minor Special Service Request (UMNR) handling at SINHUB or line stations follows GSM Chapter 2.11
  • Stations are to retain a copy of this Self-Assessment Checklist together with the UM documents for 7 years.

At Check-in:
Pax is between 5 and 17 years old (inclusive) according to the DOB in the passport.
UMNR service is confirmed, and parent/guardian has verified UMNR details.
(If the parent/guardian is not present with the UM at check-in, check-in agent must establish contact with the parent/guardian.)
Confirmed with parent/guardian if UMNR's final destination matches the system's records, UMNR has no onward connection with OAL.
If UMNR has onward connection with OAL,
Verified that parent/guardian have arranged for the UMNR to be met and cared for at the transfer point.
(A copy of confirmation with OAL is to be retained, where possible.)
Documents:
UM Service Request Form completed and properly filled in.
UM Folder has been raised.
Baggage tagged with JCL priority tag along with destination tag.
Affixed a self-adhesive orange and white candy-striped sticker to the UMNR's boarding pass and checked baggage.
Payment has been made if UM is aged between 12 and 17 years old (inclusive).
UMNR along with UM Folder has been handed over to ground staff assigned to assist UMNR through departure formalities.
If UMNR service is waived:
Pax is aged between 12 and 17 years old (inclusive).
UM Service Waiver Form completed.
