NOTES
  1. Unaccompanied Minor (UMNR) Service only applies to flights operated by Singapore Airlines (SIA) and Scoot and must be arranged separately for the outward & return journey.
  2. UMNR Service is mandatory on flights operated by SIA for minors aged between 5 to under 12 years old travelling alone or travelling in a different cabin class from their accompanying adult.
  3. Minors under 5 years old are not allowed to travel unaccompanied on flights operated by SIA. Minors under 12 years old are not allowed to travel unaccompanied on flights operated by Scoot.
  4. On a mixed itinerary with flights operated by both SIA and Scoot, minors under 12 years old will not be allowed to travel unaccompanied on both airlines.
  5. Where there is more than 1 flight sector for the journey with the flights operated by SIA, Scoot or both, the UMNR Service for Unaccompanied Minors between 12 years and below 18 years old shall be provided for the entire journey and not only for a specific flight sector in the journey.
  6. Dispensation for UMNR Service can be obtained for Unaccompanied Minors between 12 years and below 18 years old if parental/legal guardian consent is given. In such an instance, an "Unaccompanied Minor Service Waiver Form" must be signed by the parent/guardian for each outward and return journey.
  7. Carriage of Unaccompanied Minors shall be subject to SIA's General Conditions of Carriage (GCC) and Conditions of Contract (COC), which can be found on www.singaporeair.com and they shall prevail to govern SIA's rights and remedies.
  8. Carriage of Unaccompanied Minors on Scoot shall be subject to Scoot's Conditions of Carriage, which can be found on www.flyscoot.com and they shall prevail to govern Scoot's rights and remedies.
